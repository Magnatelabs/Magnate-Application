About Us

Magnate is a community-driven alternative asset manager and an investment education tool. We give business-savvy users access to alternative asset classes that they would typically be prohibited from investing in. In turn, they educate other users by investing via the support of our community-driven platform. Magnate was built by a team that has advised investment professionals with several hundred billion in assets, developed financial software for the top banks and designed the Global Citizen social network.
