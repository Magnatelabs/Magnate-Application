Our Story

They would often provide advice and guidance to friends that were hesitant about crowdfunding an interesting project or idea.

Sometimes it would be because they weren’t sure if the project timeline made sense. Other times it’d be because of confusion about the idea and if it was really plausible or useful. And many times it would be because they had already had a bad experience.

So in November 2012, a few friends hailing from New York City and the Silicon Valley got an idea: what if there was a way to empower the average crowdfunder? Make the experience more accessible by offering individualized guidance and similar in-depth portfolio management usually reserved for high-net-worth individuals. After all, the group had experience advising investment professionals managing over $60 billion in assets, creating financial instruments for the top wealth management firms and building successful web applications.

And so Magnate was born. Magnate’s mission is to make crowdfunding a more fun, more rewarding experience. Use Magnate to educate yourself on industries you’re interested in, get tips from industry veterans, and verify project progress without lifting a finger. Enjoy the perks of being the boss.
