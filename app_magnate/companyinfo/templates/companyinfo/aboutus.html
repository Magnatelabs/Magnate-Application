Our Story

Magnate is a social networking platform dedicated to empowering the young and money-minded. It allows low net worth individuals to invest in typically off-limits alternative asset classes and increases financial literacy through exciting investing strategies. A full immersion experience into the world of high finance, Magnate is Finance 101 on steroids.

Created by three friends hailing from New York City and the Silicon Valley, Magnate's founding team has advised investment professionals managing over $107 billion in assets, created financial software for the top wealth management firms and built successful social applications.
